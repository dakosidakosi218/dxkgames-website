About DXK Games

DXK Games is an independent mobile game development studio founded with a mission to create engaging, innovative games that bring joy to players worldwide. We specialise in fast-paced, casual gaming experiences designed for mobile devices, prioritising accessibility, visual appeal and addictive gameplay mechanics.

Our core values are centred on creativity, player satisfaction and technical excellence. We believe that the best games are those that respect the player's time whilst delivering maximum engagement and fun. Every aspect of our games is crafted with care, from the initial design concept through to post-launch support.

The DXK Games team brings together passionate developers, designers and game enthusiasts committed to pushing the boundaries of mobile gaming and establishing DXK Games as a leading studio in the UK gaming industry.

City Runner

City Runner is a 2D arcade-style endless runner that challenges players to navigate through a vibrant city environment whilst avoiding obstacles and collecting coins. The game combines simple controls with intense, fast-paced action to create an addictive gaming experience.

Core Gameplay: Players control a character running through city streets, swiping left and right to avoid obstacles and collecting golden coins to build their high score. The game features progressive difficulty, leaderboards and unlockable cosmetic items.

Target Audience: City Runner is designed for casual mobile gamers aged 13-30 who enjoy quick, engaging play sessions and competitive scoring.

Platforms: Available on iOS and Android. Optimised for mobile devices with support for both phones and tablets.

British Esports Federation Partnership

DXK Games is proud to partner with the British Esports Federation, a leading organisation dedicated to promoting and supporting competitive gaming in the United Kingdom. This partnership represents our commitment to the esports ecosystem and our ambition to position City Runner within the competitive gaming community.

What This Means: The partnership with the British Esports Federation provides City Runner with access to a community of engaged gamers, competitive tournament opportunities, and alignment with industry-leading practices in esports development and support.
